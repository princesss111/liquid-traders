About Liquid

Built for the
10% mindset.

Only 10% of day traders are consistently profitable — which means most of what you see online isn't the full picture. Liquid exists because that honest conversation wasn't happening anywhere.

Day trading is a male dominated field. We're changing the ratio — and Liquid is where that starts. Women learning, experimenting with strategies, and hyping each other up every step of the way.

No one here has all the answers. We're figuring it out in real time — no sugarcoating, no gurus, just honest conversations about what's working, what isn't, and what we're still trying to understand.
